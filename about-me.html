About Me

A journey from coding passion to data-driven problem-solving.

Hello! I'm Divyanshu Mishra, a graduate student pursuing my Master of Science in Information Technology and Management at the University of Texas at Dallas. With a passion for data analytics and business intelligence, I specialize in uncovering actionable insights from data to drive impactful decision-making.

I bring over 3.5 years of professional experience from Tata Consultancy Services, where I led teams to optimize banking systems and leveraged analytics tools like Power BI, Splunk and Grafana to enhance system performance. My journey includes managing databases, creating interactive dashboards, and executing data-driven projects with tools like Power BI, Tableau, and Python.

Beyond work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, mentoring aspiring data enthusiasts, and occasionally indulging in gaming (ask me about my CS:GO and Valorant adventures!).

If you're looking for someone with a knack for blending technical expertise with strategic thinking, feel free to connect with me or explore my portfolio and projects. I'm always excited to collaborate on challenging opportunities!
